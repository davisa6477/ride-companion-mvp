# Phase 28 Visual Fixes Bundle

## Home

The driver photo/top identity area is fixed at the top of the Meet card. Longer bio/local-tip text scrolls below it, while quick action buttons remain anchored at the bottom.

## Local

The Local page now separates the left category section from the right QR/search panel:

```txt
Left category section: internal scroll
Right QR panel: static card size
```

The QR panel no longer scrolls together with the category list.

## Deals

Interior ad cards now use wrapping/overflow-safe classes so long business names, headlines, descriptions, and categories stay inside the card.

The Deals page also uses the standard fixed page-frame card with internal scrolling.

## Flights

The Flight Checker page now uses the standard page-frame card and internal content scrolling.
